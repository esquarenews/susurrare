Questions, answered.

Everything you need to know before you start talking.

Do I need my own OpenAI API key?

Yes. Susurrare uses your OpenAI API key for transcription and stores it locally on your machine.

Can I switch hotkeys?

Yes. Push-to-talk, toggle recording, cancel, and mode switching are all configurable.

Where does the text go?

Susurrare inserts at your cursor or copies to your clipboard, depending on your mode settings.

Is Windows support available?

Windows support is in progress. The core is built with Windows-ready adapters.
